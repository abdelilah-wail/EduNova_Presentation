Item Response Theory Targets the Zone of Proximal Development

A 1-parameter Rasch model estimates each learner's latent ability θ from their answer history, and selects the next question whose difficulty b falls inside the ZPD — the band where success probability sits between 0.5 and 0.75.

1PL Rasch Model · Item Characteristic Curve

P = 1 / (1 + e^−(θ − b))

AI Tier Endpoint
POST /quiz AiQuizRequestDto
  • Stateless inference — θ + answer history sent in the request body.
  • Returns the next item, updated θ, and standard error σ.
  • Item bank pre-calibrated; no ground-truth labels required at runtime.
  • Quiz terminates automatically when σ < threshold.

Result: shorter quizzes, sharper ability estimates, and questions that always sit in the learner's productive struggle zone.
