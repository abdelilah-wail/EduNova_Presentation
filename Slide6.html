Why does my model overfit when I add more layers?
Good question — and it ties directly to section 3.2 of this module. Adding depth increases capacity, so without enough data or regularization the network memorizes the training set instead of generalizing.

Three things help: dropout, weight decay, and early stopping — all covered in the next quiz.
