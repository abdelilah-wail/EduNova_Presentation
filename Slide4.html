An AI-Driven Platform That Adapts in Real Time

EduNova AI continuously monitors learner behavior and performance, personalizes content delivery on the fly, modulates assessment difficulty per learner, and detects dropout risk before students disengage. Three core mechanisms drive everything.

Three Core Mechanisms
Pillar 01
Behavioral Analysis Engine
Captures session frequency, task completion, and error patterns as first-class events.
Pillar 02
Adaptive Quiz Module (IRT)
Item Response Theory difficulty ladder — every question targeted to the learner's current ability.
Pillar 03
Dropout-Risk Predictor
Quantifies abandonment probability per session and triggers instructor alerts before disengagement.
