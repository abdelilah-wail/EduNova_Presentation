Privacy by Design — Built on Four Non-Negotiable Pillars

Personalization at this depth only works if learners trust the system with their words and emotions. Every signal we use is governed by four pillars covering what we collect, where it runs, who can call it, and how learners reclaim it.

Pillar 01

Data Minimization

Only the signals required for personalization are stored. Raw chat is held in a bounded sliding window; sentiment and emotion are kept as aggregated scores, never as labeled transcripts.

N = 12 turns aggregates only
✓ Aligned GDPR Art. 5(1)(c)
Pillar 02

On-Premise Affect AI

Sentiment and emotion models run locally on the institution's server. Learner text never leaves the campus boundary for affect analysis — no third-party cloud sees raw chat content.

FastAPI :8001 DistilRoBERTa
✓ Aligned GDPR Art. 44
Pillar 03

Strict Access Boundary

The Python service is not exposed to the internet. All AI calls flow through the .NET BFF, gated by JWT authentication and role checks from UserTypeEnum.

JWT AIController.cs RBAC
✓ Aligned GDPR Art. 32
Pillar 04

Right to Forget

Learners can wipe their AI memory at any time — chat history, affect aggregates, and θ estimates. Deletion is cascaded across services and recorded in the audit log.

DELETE /ai/memory cascade audit
✓ Aligned GDPR Art. 17
